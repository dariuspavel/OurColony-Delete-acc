App: Our Colony • Developer: Golden Desert • Effective:

How to request deletion

  1. In-app: Settings → Account → Delete My Data. This removes your account and all associated cloud saves immediately.
  2. By email: Email us at goldendesert.studio@gmail.com from the address linked to your account. Include your player ID (if known) and “Delete Account” in the subject.
Email deletion request

What we delete

  • Account and authentication record (OAuth/UGS Auth link).
  • Cloud Save data (progress, currencies, settings, unlocks).
  • Any other user-provided data stored by the app.

What we may retain

  • Security logs/anti-fraud records (if any) for up to 30 days.
  • Aggregated, non-identifiable analytics (if collected).

Processing time

  • In-app deletion: immediate.
  • Email requests: within 30 days.

Encryption in transit

All communication between the app and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS.

Identity verification

We may request additional information to confirm ownership before completing a deletion request initiated via email.
